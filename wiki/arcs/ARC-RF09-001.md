## What This Is

Melissa systematically distorts documented reality in her communications — denying things she clearly said, accusing Eli of behaviors she exhibits, and amplifying or fabricating events. The dominant form is **DARVO-inversion gaslighting**: she makes false accusations, then when challenged, reverses the claim to accuse Eli of the same behavior.

**Documented mechanisms:**
1. **Threat denial** — issuing threats then denying she made them
2. **Event amplification** — real events progressively distorted to false characterizations
3. **DARVO inversion** — accusing Eli of behaviors (harassment, aggression, gaslighting) that are documented in her own communications
4. **Level A cross-reference** — confirmed pair where the same event is described completely differently by each party

## The Phase C Engine: February 2026

The most significant gaslighting event in the dataset is the February 2026 incident rewrite — a documented 5-step amplification from acknowledged real event to false characterization:

3. Olivia was traumatized by witnessing it (escalation)

This fabrication became the stated justification for the Phase C visit-refusal cascade. The fact that the underlying real event was acknowledged makes the amplification chain **documentable in Melissa's own messages**.
