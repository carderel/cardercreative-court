## What This Arc Documents

Three documented mechanisms that block Eli's phone calls to Olivia:
1. **DND infrastructure** on Melissa's phone — unilaterally installed, moving-target cutoff, selectively lifted
2. **Child preference relay** — "She said she doesn't want to talk"
3. **Direct personal blocking** — Melissa admits blocking Eli's calls herself (ROW-2639)

---

## Three Blocking Mechanisms

### Mechanism 1: DND Infrastructure

Melissa installed a Do Not Disturb cutoff that routes Eli's calls to voicemail after a set time. Key properties:
- **Moving target:** Originally 8:30pm, later shifted to 9:30pm (Oct 2024, ROW-7581)
- **Selectively lifted:** Lifted when convenient (vacation, Eli answered "on time"), enforced as punishment post-conflict
- **On Melissa's phone, not Olivia's:** Confirmed — the blocking infrastructure is on Melissa's device, meaning Melissa controls it even when Olivia might want to talk
- **20+ month continuity:** DND confirmed operational from Sep 2022 through May 2024 (ROW-5264-5266)

### Mechanism 2: Child Preference Relay

*"She said she doesn't want to talk"* — repeated across the dataset as access closure. Because ROW-6819 confirmed Melissa coaches Olivia's communication, these relay statements must be evaluated against the coaching mechanism.

### Mechanism 3: Direct Personal Blocking

**ROW-2639 (Jun 6, 2023):** *"No I did. We were in the middle of playing Roblox"* — Melissa admits she personally sent Eli's call to voicemail, not Olivia.

## Selective Enforcement (5 Documented Instances)

The DND rule is **selectively enforced** — lifted when convenient, tightened post-conflict. This demonstrates it is not a principled time-of-night rule but a gating mechanism:
1. ROW-1186 (B-004): "I'll take the do not disturb off if you wanna call her in 5 minutes"
2. ROW-2550 (B-007): "If you called at 8:30 my do not disturb is on. I'll turn it off since she can sleep in"
3. ROW-2899 (B-008): "I don't care what time you call this week or next"
4. ROW-3006 (B-009): "I told you last week that there's no curfew cause I'm on vacation"
5. ROW-3009 (B-009): DND was active when Eli called; Melissa confirms she manually disabled it an hour prior
