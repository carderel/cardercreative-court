## The Projection Component

ARC-RF15-002 (Systematic Projection) documents the specific pattern where Melissa accuses Eli of behaviors that are documented in her own communications. The four confirmed projection pairs are:

1. Accuses Eli of **gaslighting** — documented gaslighting by Melissa
2. Accuses Eli of **making false allegations** — she is the documented false-allegation source
3. Accuses Eli of **coaching Olivia** — she explicitly admitted coaching Olivia (ROW-6819)
4. Accuses Eli of **exposing Olivia to adult conflict** — documented in RF-11 signals

The projection pattern transforms the false allegations into a double function: making the allegations AND preemptively deflecting Eli's ability to raise the same concerns.
