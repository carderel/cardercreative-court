## What This Is

Beginning in Phase B (post-police-report period), Melissa's communication style shifted from overt hostility to strategic documentation framing. Messages became more measured in tone while deploying specific language designed to create a favorable paper trail.

The primary mechanism is the **strategic disclaimer**: a negative characterization delivered immediately followed by a preemptive claim of neutral intent. The structure: *[accusation or attack]* + *"I'm not trying to start anything, that's just the truth."*

This pattern:
1. Delivers the harmful content (accusation, comparison, criticism)
2. Preemptively defuses challenge by claiming neutral intent
3. Creates a record that Eli was "informed" of the problem
4. Makes it difficult to characterize the message as hostile
