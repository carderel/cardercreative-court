## What This Is

This arc tracks the overall trajectory of conflict escalation across the dataset — not individual episodes but the **worsening trend** over time. The trajectory shows:

- **Phase A:** Escalation onset; 46 hostile episodes documented; foundational patterns established
- **Phase B:** Eli adopts AI-guided communication (May 4, 2025); hostile episode density continues regardless
- **Phase C:** Structural shift — overt hostility moderates but is replaced by systematic access-denial machinery (visit refusals, legal action)

**Key finding:** Escalation did not stop in Phase C — it **changed form**. The loud, emotionally volatile hostility of Phase A gave way to the quieter, more legally consequential access-denial campaign of Phase C.

## Key Finding: AI-Guided Communication Test (Phase B)

Beginning May 4, 2025, Eli adopted AI-guided communication — measurably more de-escalatory messages. **Melissa's escalation behavior did not change.** This is a controlled observation: Eli's behavior changed; Melissa's did not. This confirms that Melissa's hostile behavior is not a response to Eli's communication style — it is self-generated.

This is directly relevant to any court evaluation of whether Eli is "causing" the conflict.
