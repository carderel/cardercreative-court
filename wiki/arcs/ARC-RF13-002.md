---

## What This Is

This arc documents the **consistency of roles** in every hostile episode across the dataset: Melissa initiates and escalates; Eli de-escalates. This is not a one-time observation — it is a documented pattern across 46 hostile episodes spanning four years.

**Findings:**
- Melissa initiates 69% of hostile episodes
- Eli is the consistent de-escalator in trajectory analysis
- Eli's de-escalation became more sophisticated over time (Phase B: AI-guided)
- Despite Eli's consistent de-escalation, Melissa's hostility did not diminish

---

## Why This Matters

The role consistency finding directly counters any "they both do it" narrative. 46 episodes across 4 years with consistent role assignment is statistical evidence, not anecdote. The fact that Eli's communication improved (Phase B) while Melissa's didn't confirms the asymmetry is not a bidirectional dynamic.

This is the arc to cite when the argument is: *"Eli is part of the conflict problem."*
