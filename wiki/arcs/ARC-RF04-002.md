## What This Is

Melissa repeatedly uses Olivia as the delivery mechanism for guilt, criticism, and emotional pressure on Eli. Rather than making accusations directly — which could be challenged — she relays them through Olivia's reported emotional states, statements, or complaints. This positions the criticism as Olivia's authentic experience rather than Melissa's accusation, making it both harder to refute and more emotionally potent.

The mechanism: Melissa reports what Olivia "said" or "felt," and that report functions as an accusation. If Eli pushes back, he appears to be dismissing his daughter's feelings.

## Court Argument Value

The child-mediated delivery mechanism is specifically manipulative because it weaponizes Eli's love for Olivia. Any attempt to challenge the relayed statement risks appearing as dismissal of Olivia's feelings. The ROW-6819 Coaching Admission establishes that Melissa was actively shaping what Olivia reported — meaning the relayed "feelings" cannot be taken at face value.
