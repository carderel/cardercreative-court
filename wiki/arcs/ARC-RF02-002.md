## What This Arc Documents

Melissa's construction and enforcement of a rule that **all Eli-Olivia contact must route through her**. Over 64 documented signals across 3.5 years, the rule expanded from scheduling to affective, therapeutic, safety, and daily-behavior communication domains. By Feb 2025, Olivia herself was enforcing the rule.

---

## Normalization Trajectory

The norm migrated across three carriers:

1. **Melissa's external enforcement** (2022-2023): Direct assertions, objections to Eli's unilateral contact with Olivia
2. **Eli's internalized compliance** (2024): Eli pre-enforces the rule with Olivia before Melissa needs to (ROW-7206, ROW-12439)
3. **Olivia's behavioral implementation** (Feb 2025, ROW-9096): Olivia redirects Eli to seek Melissa's approval unprompted

**This is the normalization endpoint** — the access-control architecture has become ambient to all three parties.
