## What This Is

Melissa systematically uses Olivia as a communication relay — passing messages, complaints, emotional pressure, and compliance demands through Olivia rather than directly. This triangulates the child into adult conflict, burdens her with adult-level emotional weight, and creates a mechanism Eli cannot challenge without appearing to dismiss Olivia's feelings.

The arc documents two phases of escalation:
1. **Intelligence gathering** — Olivia reports on Eli's household; Melissa mines those reports and relays them back to Eli as accusations
2. **Compliance delivery** — Olivia's voiced preferences used to communicate Melissa's demands (visit refusals, Abby exclusion, grievance lists)

---

## The Coaching Admission

**ROW-6819 (Aug 21, 2024)** is the structural anchor for this entire arc. Melissa explicitly stated: *"I've told her to have conversations and I also give her things to tell you about. I remind her of some of the things she's told me."*

This admission confirms that what Olivia "said" to Eli was not independent — it was coached, directed, or reminded. Every preference relay and complaint relay in the arc must be evaluated through this lens.
