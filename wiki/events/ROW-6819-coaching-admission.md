## Why This Is the Most Structurally Significant Message in the Dataset

This is an **explicit, unprompted, undenied admission** that Melissa:
1. **Scripts** the topics of Olivia's conversations with Eli (*"I've told her to have conversations"*)
2. **Provides content** for Olivia to relay (*"I give her things to tell you about"*)
3. **Mines** Olivia's reports about Eli's household for information (*"I remind her of some of the things she's told me"*)

All three behaviors are described in the first person, present tense, without qualification. The message was not retracted or denied in the remainder of the dataset.

## Structural Implications

Every Olivia-attributed statement that follows this message in the dataset — preference relays, fear claims, visit-refusal statements, complaint reports — must be evaluated against this confirmed coaching mechanism.

**This does not mean Olivia has no genuine feelings.** It means the channel through which those feelings are communicated to Eli (Melissa's relay) is confirmed to be curated and primed.
