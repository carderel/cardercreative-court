# Key Event: November 22, 2025 Police Report

**Date:** November 22, 2025
**Batch:** B-029/B-030

## What Happened

Eli filed a police report on November 22, 2025. Olivia refused to come for Eli's scheduled custody weekend. Melissa was aware of Olivia's refusal and did not intervene to enforce compliance.

---

## Why This Is a Key Event

This is the **first formal legal action Eli took** in response to the visit-refusal cascade. It represents a threshold event in Phase C — Eli escalated from requesting compliance to involving law enforcement.

---

## Melissa's Response — DARVO (COMP-RF04-028)

**Nov 25, 2025 (ROW-14851):** Melissa characterizes Eli's police report as the basis for "a big contempt case" against Eli — inverting the victim/offender positioning. Eli filed a report about denied access; Melissa reframed it as Eli's contempt. This is the definitional DARVO response: Eli's legal action → Eli as wrongdoer.

---

## Police Report Disclosed to Olivia (RF-11)

Melissa disclosed to Olivia that Eli filed a police report. This is classified as the **highest child-welfare-consequence event** in RF-11 — a child being told her father called police regarding her. The disclosure creates adult-conflict burden directly in the child.

---

## Gatekeeping-to-Contempt Inversion (RF-05, S-025)

Melissa characterized Eli's police report as *him* violating court orders — a "gatekeeping-to-contempt inversion." The mechanism: Eli asserts his legal rights under the parenting plan → Melissa reframes this as Eli's breach → threat of contempt action against Eli.

---

## Context: Visit-Refusal Cascade at This Point

By November 22, 2025, the visit-refusal cascade was ongoing:
- Aug 26–Sep 5: First multi-day cascade
- Oct 10: Full weekend cancelled
- Nov 7-8: "5 years" duration claim + physical threshold refusal
- Nov 22: Police report filed

The police report was filed after approximately three months of consecutive visit refusals — including one with documented physical threshold refusal.

---

## Phase C Significance

The police report filing is the inflection point that separates early Phase C (escalating relays) from late Phase C (formal legal positioning on both sides). After this date:
- Melissa's communications become more legally framed
- RF-12 (strategic communication) post-police-report tone shift confirmed (ADD-004)
- Legal escalation continues through Phase C
