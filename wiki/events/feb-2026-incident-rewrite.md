*The following is Eli's direct account of the incident, relevant to GAL prep and attorney briefing.*

**The morning:** Olivia wanted donuts. Eli had purchased muffin ingredients to make with her instead. When he went to the kitchen, it was dirty — he had to clean before he could even start cooking. Running on no sleep after an ER visit, managing an 8-month chronic medical condition, and faced with a messy kitchen when he was trying to do something for Olivia, he snapped. He began yelling at Abby about his frustration with the state of the household and the lack of help he felt he was receiving.

**Who was present:** Abby, Olivia, Milo (7), and Alice (17 months). The children were in Olivia's room during the argument.

Eli said he understood and preferred she stay, but would not stop her. He helped her get ready. When she left, she hugged him and told him she loved him.

**What it was not:** There was no alcohol involved. No physical contact. The argument was with Abby about household frustrations, not directed at Olivia. The children were in another room.
