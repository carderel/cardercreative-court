## Summary

ROW-2401 provides Melissa's version: Abby made the comment in response to Olivia's threat to report, which Olivia interpreted as a punishment for telling the truth. Even if Abby's comment was unfortunate, Melissa relaying 'she won't go' as a standing visit condition — rather than addressing the specific incident — is the RF-02 behavior.
