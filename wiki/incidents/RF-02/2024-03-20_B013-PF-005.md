## Summary

Eli asks if Olivia wants to be picked up. Melissa responds by immediately imposing specific homework requirements as the implicit condition of the visit ('home by 9 with half of her homework done... 6 units of Lexia need to be completed'). Unlike the third instance (ROW-4539: 'If not she can't go'), the explicit denial-contingency language is absent — but the structural pattern is the same: visit access conditioned on homework compliance communicated at the moment of access request. This is the fourth homework-conditional-access event in approximately nine weeks (Jan 18 – Mar 20, 2024). The pattern is now sufficiently established to track as a distinct sub-pattern within ARC-RF02-002.
