She doesn't deserve that'), characterizes Eli as a habitual FaceTime-vaper (ROW-3144: 'I've seen you do it on FaceTime with her before'), and channels Olivia's anger toward Eli in a sustained way ('she's still mad at you,' ROW-3151; demanding apology, ROW-3158).
