RF-15 primary (false allegation / predatory implication). Near-boundary RF-01: the logical corollary is 'Eli's apparent engagement with Olivia was not genuine.' The 'I really hope' framing gives Melissa deniability ('I was just expressing concern') while planting the suggestion.
