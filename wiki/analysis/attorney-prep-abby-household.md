## 2. Relationship Origin — Full Honest Account

The attorney needs this account in full. It contains vulnerability (the affair) but also context that defuses the worst of Melissa's framing.

### Timeline
- **Late March / Early April 2020:** Abby comes to live with Eli, Melissa, and Olivia. Melissa's decision — Abby had domestic trouble with the father of her son Milo and needed to be kept away from him. Milo (~1 year old) and Olivia (~4) played together. Eli (who worked nights) and Abby watched the kids together during the day.
- **Approximately May–June 2020:** After Abby attended court regarding her ex, she returned and had drinks (she was 20, a few months from 21; Eli offered access to household alcohol without concern). She became intoxicated and confessed having a crush on Eli. She then became ill. Eli and Melissa both helped her to bed. **Nothing physical occurred that night.**

- **On or around July 15, 2020:** Melissa kicked Eli out of the marital home (stated reason: vaping). When Eli left, Abby left with him. That is when Melissa discovered the affair.
- **July 2021:** Divorce filed.
- **September 2024:** Alice born — Eli and Abby's daughter.

- Redirect to the present: this is a 6-year committed relationship that produced a child; the question for the evaluator/attorney is the stability and safety of the current household, not the circumstances of how the relationship began
