## Relationship Origin — Full Account

Abby came to live with Eli, Melissa, and Olivia in late March or early April 2020, after experiencing domestic trouble with the father of her son Milo (who was approximately 1 at the time, Olivia was approximately 4). Melissa and Eli agreed to take Abby in to keep her safely away from her ex. Eli worked nights; during the day he watched Olivia while Abby watched Milo. The children played together and Eli and Abby spent time together supervising them.

Approximately two to three months after Abby arrived, she attended court regarding issues with her ex. When she returned, she had a drink (she was 20 years old; Eli offered her access to the household's alcohol, not thinking anything of it given she was nearly 21). She became intoxicated and confessed to having a crush on Eli. Shortly after, she became ill from the alcohol and was helped to bed — Melissa was present and helped. Nothing physical occurred that night.

The following day, sober, Abby and Eli talked about the confession. That conversation was the start of the relationship.

Eli was kicked out of the marital home on or around July 15, 2020 — ostensibly for vaping. When he left, Abby left with him. That is when Melissa discovered the affair.

**Key facts for attorney use:**
- The affair began while Eli was still married to Melissa and while the marriage was already in serious trouble
- Abby was 20 years old when the relationship began; the age gap between Eli and Abby is 22 years
- Both are legal adults; no legal issue with the relationship itself
- Melissa brought Abby into the home — the origin of the relationship flows from Melissa's own decision
- Melissa was present the night of the drunk confession and helped Abby to bed
