# Olivia — Behavioral Profile

**Role:** Child subject of custody proceedings — Case No. 21-DR-1773
**Age:** Approx. born 2016-2017 (approximately 8-9 at dataset start; 9-10 at dataset close)
**Dataset role:** Instrument of access-control mechanisms; subject of parentification and coaching findings

---

## Overview

Olivia appears in the dataset primarily through **Melissa's relay claims** about her preferences, fears, and wishes. The coaching admission (ROW-6819) means that Olivia's relayed statements cannot be taken at face value without cross-referencing direct interactions. The analysis documents how Olivia's agency has been progressively instrumentalized — from Melissa's early relay claims, to Olivia's own enforcement of the required-intermediary norm, to her physical refusal to cross Eli's threshold in Phase C.

**Critical methodological note:** Every "Olivia said" relay in Melissa's messages must be evaluated against the confirmed coaching mechanism.

---

## Key Findings Involving Olivia

### Required-Intermediary Normalization (RF-02 / ARC-RF02-002)
By February 14, 2025 (ROW-9096), Olivia herself redirected Eli to seek Melissa's approval when asked about a Valentine's Day pickup — *"She said ask you."* This is the normalization endpoint: the access-control norm has migrated from Melissa's enforcement → Eli's compliance → **Olivia's behavioral implementation**.

### Parentification of Visit-Compliance Decision (RF-11 / ARC-RF11-001)
The dominant mechanism in RF-11. Melissa positions Olivia's weekend-visit decisions as entirely Olivia's choice:
- ROW-14841: *"It was her choice"* (Melissa disclaims responsibility for non-visits)
- This places Olivia in the role of the parent who decides custody compliance — a clinically significant burden for a child
- Eli cannot correct the situation without appearing to override Olivia's "choice"

### Police Report Disclosure (RF-11)
Melissa disclosed to Olivia that Eli filed a police report on November 22, 2025. The analysis identifies this as the **highest child-welfare-consequence event** in RF-11 — exposing a child to the fact that her father called police regarding her constitutes a significant parenting decision that heightens adult conflict.

### Physical Threshold Refusal (Phase C)
First documented physical refusal to enter Eli's custody: November 8, 2025 (ROW-8424): *"Call her cause she refuses to come out. It was a mess to get her out of the car even."* Subsequent instances: Feb 27, 2026 (ROW-8576): *"You can come but she says she's not coming out."*

### Olivia as Emotional Stabilizer (CP2 Finding)
Eli confirmed at CP2 that Olivia serves as an emotional stabilizer in Melissa's household — an adult role. This is a parentification concern independent of the visit-compliance dimension.

### Therapist Pattern
Olivia has had therapists. Melissa drops therapists when they challenge her narrative — this is the pattern of concern in any custody evaluation, not whether Olivia is in therapy.

### Counselor Relay Weaponization (RF-11)
ROW-14097 (Aug 14, 2025): Melissa weaponizes Olivia's counseling session — uses counselor-relayed language about Olivia being afraid of Eli to reinforce the required-intermediary and gatekeeping positions.

## Phase C Trajectory

Olivia's access behavior in Phase C mirrors Melissa's relay claims with increasing intensity:
- Aug-Sep 2025: First multi-day refusals relayed
- Nov 2025: "5 years" duration claim relayed; physical threshold refusal documented
- Feb-Mar 2026: "Traumatized" language introduced; indefinite weekend-pause claim
- Apr 9, 2026: Olivia ultimately attends Eli's weekend through Melissa-mediated brokering — demonstrates that Olivia-Eli relationship is not irreparably broken when the intermediary structure permits

## Abby Relationship

Olivia is Abby's cousin (Abby is Eli's niece; Eli and Abby are in a relationship). Melissa uses this relationship as an attack vector. In April 2023, visit access was conditioned on Abby's absence (Melissa relayed Olivia's "preference"). In February 2026, a household incident involving Abby and Eli arguing became the stated catalyst for Olivia's visit refusal cascade — the incident was real, the amplification was not.
