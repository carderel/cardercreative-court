## Exact SPP Language

> Neither parent shall introduce the child to a **significant other** unless the parties have been in an exclusive relationship for at least **nine (9) months**.
>
> After the nine-month threshold is met, the significant other shall only have **supervised contact** with the child for an additional **24 months** — for a total of **33 months** before unsupervised access is permitted.

The SPP creates a 33-month pathway before a significant other has unsupervised access to Olivia. The purpose is child welfare protection.

## Why Gary's DV History Matters

The 9-month rule and supervised-intro requirement are child-protective provisions. Their purpose is to prevent a child from being exposed to an unknown adult in an unsupervised household context before that adult's character and stability can be assessed.

Gary's 2023 domestic violence arrest is precisely the type of risk the SPP's significant other provisions are designed to protect against. His DV history is not a peripheral fact — it is the core reason the court would have required the supervised introduction protocol to be followed.
