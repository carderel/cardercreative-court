# §III.D — Communication Rights Violations

**SPP Reference:** Section III.D — Contact / Telephone and Electronic Communication
**Violation Type:** Interference with Eli's telephone and electronic access to Olivia
**Documented Violations:** 91 combined signals (ARC-RF02-001: 27 phone-blocking + ARC-RF02-002: 64 required-intermediary)

---

## Exact SPP Language

> Each parent is **entitled to reasonable telephone and electronic communication** with the child without interference from the other parent.
>
> The parent in possession of the child **shall not interfere with, stop, monitor, or record** any telephone or electronic communication between the child and the other parent.
>
> The parent in possession shall **timely facilitate a return telephone call** from the child to the other parent.

Three independent obligations. Each is violated in the documented record.

---

## Violation 1: Phone Blocking (ARC-RF02-001 — 27 signals)

Melissa uses Do Not Disturb (DND) on her phone to unilaterally control when Eli can reach Olivia. The mechanism has three layers:

1. **DND infrastructure:** DND set at 8:30pm — Eli's calls during evening hours go unanswered. Melissa selectively lifts DND to control contact timing.
2. **Preference relay as DND cover:** "She doesn't want to talk" relays occur in conjunction with DND, masking the blocking mechanism.
3. **Direct personal blocking:** ROW-2639 — Melissa admitted blocking Eli's personal number.

**Duration:** Confirmed continuous from Sep 2022 (B-001) through May 2024 (B-015) — 20+ months of documented blocking. Final arc signal: Apr 1, 2026 — Melissa witnesses Olivia flip Eli to voicemail and does not intervene.

## Violation 2: Required Intermediary (ARC-RF02-002 — 64 signals)

The most extensively documented arc in the entire dataset. All Eli-Olivia contact must route through Melissa. This is a structural violation of the SPP's communication entitlement.

### Melissa's Own Admissions

**ROW-4383-4385 (Jan 24, 2024):**
> *"She normally only calls because I ask if she wants to or I just dial you. I didn't ask her so she didn't call. Once in awhile she asks on her own."*

This is a direct admission that Eli's communication with his daughter is conditional on Melissa initiating it. "Reasonable telephone communication without interference" means Eli can call his daughter — not that Melissa must decide whether to dial for him.

**ROW-3151 (Jul 31, 2023):**
> *"I normally ask her if she wants to call you in the am. I'm the reason she calls you most days."*

## Violation 3: Failure to Timely Facilitate Return Calls

The SPP requires the parent in possession to "timely facilitate a return telephone call." The required-intermediary architecture is the structural opposite of facilitation — it is selective facilitation controlled entirely by Melissa's discretion. Calls return only when Melissa decides to initiate them.
