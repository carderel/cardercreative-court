## The Billing-on-Refused-Visits Pattern

The childcare receipt log should be read alongside the RF-06 incident files. The pattern:
1. Melissa refuses Eli's parenting time (RF-02 signal)
2. Melissa incurs childcare costs because the visit that would have covered that time was refused
3. Melissa bills Eli for those childcare costs

This means Eli is financially penalized for asserting his parenting rights. The receipt log + the RF-02 visit-refusal record together document this pattern.
