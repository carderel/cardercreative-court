## What This Pattern Covers

Parental alienation: communications that undermine Olivia's relationship with Eli, portray Eli negatively to Olivia, or create psychological barriers to the father-child relationship.

---

## Key Finding

ARC-RF01-001 carries 27 signals spanning the full dataset, with the **last signal in August 2025** (B-028). Phase C (Sep 2025 – Apr 2026) produced **zero new primary RF-01 signals** — but this does not mean alienation ceased. The finding is that **Melissa's RF-01 delivery mechanism shifted**: direct alienating messages to Eli about Olivia were replaced by the visit-refusal cascade (RF-02) as the primary vehicle. The coaching admission (ROW-6819) means Olivia continues receiving messages Eli does not see.

---

## Arc Narrative Components

The alienation narrative has five documented sub-components, all of which appear in relayed statements, direct accusations to Eli, and third-party reach:

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Abandonment / inadequate-father** | Framing Eli as absent, uncaring, prioritizing other things over Olivia |
| **Abby-prioritization** | Eli chooses Abby over Olivia; Abby comes first in the household |
| **Abby-as-obstacle** | Abby is the reason Olivia can't have a normal relationship with Eli |
| **Untrustworthy-father** | Eli cannot be relied upon, lies, breaks promises |
| **Predatory-father / false allegation** | Sexual predation framing via Abby relationship; "unsafe" framing |

## Phase A vs. Phase C Shift

- **Phase A–B:** Melissa delivers RF-01 content directly (accusations to Eli about his parenting, statements about Olivia's view of Eli)
- **Phase C:** RF-01 content is embedded in visit-refusal relays ("she's traumatized," "she doesn't want to see you for 5 years") — harder to isolate as RF-01 alone because it's compound with RF-02
- **B-028 (Aug 2025):** Last explicit primary RF-01 signal — comprehensive ADD-004 relay at the visit-refusal cascade onset
