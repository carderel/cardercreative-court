## What This Pattern Covers

Triangulation: using Olivia as a communication vehicle, informant, or emotional intermediary in the co-parenting conflict. Child coaching: scripting or priming Olivia's statements to Eli.

---

## The Foundational Event: ROW-6819

**August 21, 2024 — ROW-6819 (B-018):**
*"I've told her to have conversations and I also give her things to tell you about. I remind her of some of the things she's told me."*

This is the **most structurally significant single message in the dataset**. It is an explicit, unprompted, undenied admission that Melissa:
1. Scripts the topics of Olivia's conversations with Eli
