## AI-Guided Communication and De-Escalation Resistance

On May 4, 2025 (EP-1078), Eli began using AI-guided communication to ensure his messages were measured and non-escalatory. Melissa's escalation behavior continued without change. This is the definitional criterion for **de-escalation resistance** — the hostile-episode pattern persisted regardless of Eli's communication posture.

This finding is critical for attributing responsibility for the conflict dynamic: Eli's behavior demonstrably changed; Melissa's did not.

## Trigger Unpredictability

The analysis confirmed **trigger unpredictability** as a documented feature of the escalation pattern. Hostile episodes are not reliably tied to specific precipitating events — the unpredictability creates a walking-on-eggshells dynamic where Eli cannot predict what will produce hostile exchange.
