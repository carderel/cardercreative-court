## What This Pattern Covers

## Key Finding: Parentification as Dominant Mechanism

**ROW-14841: "It was her choice."**

This parentification creates an impossible position for Eli: he cannot compel the visits without appearing to override his daughter's agency, and he cannot negotiate without going through Melissa (the required-intermediary rule).

Telling a child that her father called police regarding her attendance is the highest-consequence child-exposure event in RF-11 — it places adult legal conflict directly into the child's awareness and heightens the emotional weight of the custody dispute for Olivia personally.
