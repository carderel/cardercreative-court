## What This Pattern Covers

Refusal to co-parent: behaviors that structurally prevent shared decision-making — simulated cooperation, medical record exclusion, therapist gatekeeping, and resistance to joint parenting communication.

---

## Critical Note: Low Flag Count ≠ Pattern Absence

**1 primary flag does not mean co-parenting refusal is not occurring.** CP2 directly confirmed multiple co-parenting refusal patterns that are **not keyword-identifiable in message text**. The evidence type for this pattern is behavioral, not textual.
