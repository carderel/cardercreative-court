## Key Mechanisms

### Contempt as Default Threat
The contempt threat is Melissa's first-resort legal weapon. By Nov 2022 it was deployed as the opening move in co-parenting disputes. By Phase C it escalated to specific execution dates ("filing contempt on Monday" — RF-06 cross-reference).

### Retainer-Free Disclosure (Feb 2024)
Melissa disclosed that she didn't have an attorney on retainer — initially appearing to reduce threat credibility, but analytically this **shifts the burden**: Melissa can file without any retained representation, reducing the friction cost of a filing.

### Structural Legal Access Advantage (Confirmed — CP3 review, May 2026)
Melissa has two documented structural advantages that make her legal threats more credible than the paper-tiger trajectory alone suggests:

1. **Legal access through employer benefits:** Melissa's employer provides legal services as a workplace benefit. She can consult attorneys without retainer or hourly cost — reducing the financial friction of filing to near zero.
2. **Post-divorce inheritance:** Melissa received an inheritance shortly after the divorce was finalized. Amount unknown to Eli but confirmed to be **north of $200,000**. This gives her substantial liquid resources to fund prolonged litigation if she chooses.

These two factors together explain why Melissa's threats carry behavioral weight even without execution: the structural capacity to file is real, even if the threats have not yet materialized into actual filings. **Zero modifications have been filed by Melissa as of May 2026** (confirmed by Eli, CP3-RF05-003) — but the capacity exists and is documented.

### The Rape/Assault Allegation (May 2024)
The highest-severity single legal threat in the dataset (Sev-2 compound with RF-04). Alleges criminal conduct. No formal filing documented.

### "Gatekeeping-to-Contempt" Inversion (Nov 2025, S-025)
After Eli filed a police report for visit refusal, Melissa reframed Eli's legal action as *him* violating court orders. This is the clearest example of the RF-05/RF-04 compound mechanism in Phase C.

## Impact Finding

**ADD-005:** Eli's vigilance posture — the behavioral modification sustained by the threat environment — is itself a documented harm independent of whether any individual threat was executed. The 26-threat sustained compliance environment represents 4 years of coercive influence.

**Pre-2022 gap:** Dataset gap means additional threats before August 2022 are not captured. The June 3, 2022 contempt execution establishes pre-dataset precedent.
